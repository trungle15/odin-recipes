Orange Beef Recipe: A Delicious and Easy Chinese Takeout-Inspired Dish to Make at Home

Orange beef is a delicious Chinese-American dish that combines tender slices of beef with a sweet and tangy orange sauce. It's the perfect combination of savory and sweet, and it's sure to become a new family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of orange juice
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup of diced green onions
  • 1/4 cup of diced red bell pepper

How to cook:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the orange juice, soy sauce, cornstarch, brown sugar, and garlic powder.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the beef to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until it's browned on both sides.
  4. Add the orange sauce to the skillet and bring it to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium and simmer the beef in the sauce for 5-7 minutes, or until it's cooked through.
  6. Stir in the green onions and red bell pepper and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  7. Serve the orange beef over rice or noodles and enjoy!
