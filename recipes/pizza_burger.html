Delicious Pizza Burger Recipe

This pizza burger combines two of America's favorite foods into one delicious meal. The patty is seasoned with Italian herbs and topped with marinara sauce, cheese, and pepperoni. Give this recipe a try and take your burger game to the next level!

Recipe

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce

How to cook:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the ground beef, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired level of doneness is reached.
  4. During the last minute of grilling, place a slice of cheese on top of each patty and add the pepperoni slices.
  5. Spread the marinara sauce on the bottom half of each bun. Place a patty on top of the sauce, then add the top bun.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
